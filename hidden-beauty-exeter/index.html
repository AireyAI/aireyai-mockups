Our story

A small space, run with care.

Hidden Beauty opened in 2020 — a quiet, plant-filled treatment room in Exeter, run by a mother-and-daughter team who'd rather see four clients well than fourteen in a rush.

We use products we trust, we don't sell what you don't need, and we genuinely enjoy the chat (or the silence, if that's what you're after).

Unrushed by design

Generous appointment slots so nothing feels squeezed. You leave when the treatment is finished — not when the clock says.

Clean, considered products

Cruelty-free, sensitive-skin friendly. We'll tell you when something isn't right for you — even if we stock it.

Evening appointments

Two late nights a week so you don't have to take a half-day off work just to get your brows done.

Honest pricing

Every price is on the website. No mystery "consultation" charges. If you'd rather just text, that's fine too.
