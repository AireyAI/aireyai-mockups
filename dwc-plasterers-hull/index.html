About DWC

Darren and a small crew. Nothing flash. Just walls that look right.

DWC has been plastering across Hull and the East Riding for the better part of two decades. Re-skims that hide the old artex. Full re-plasters after damp's been sorted. Skim-only finishes on new boards for the builders. Ceilings, walls, archways — whatever needs flattening before the painter comes in.

You'll see the same face on quote day as on finishing day. No subcontract crews, no "the lads will be round Tuesday." If we say Thursday at eight, we're parked outside at five to.

Tidy on the job. Off the job by the time we said. That's the whole pitch.

What we do

Six things, done properly.

Quotes are in person — five-minute look in the room beats anything we could say over text.

Skimming & re-skimming

Bumpy, blown or tired walls re-skimmed to a smooth paint-ready finish. Most rooms in a day.

Full plaster work

New boards, bonding coats, full re-plasters after damp or rewires. Honest about what each room needs.

Ceiling repair & over-board

Cracked, sagged or artex'd ceilings either over-boarded and skimmed, or pulled down and re-done.

External rendering

Sand-and-cement render, monocouche, or patch-and-blend on existing render. Scaffold sorted.

Damp patch repair

After the damp's been treated, we hack off, sand-and-cement, then skim back to flat. No shortcuts.

Coving & archways

Plain or ornate coving fitted neatly. Squared-off archways and reveals where the old plaster's blown.
