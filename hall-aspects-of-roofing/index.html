What we do

Every roof. Every detail. Done properly.

From a single slipped slate to a full council reroof, the work is scoped in writing, priced in writing and signed off on completion. No hidden extras.

Pitched roofs

Natural slate, concrete tile and clay pantile. Full strip-and-relays, partial repairs, ridge re-bedding, valley relining.

Flat roofs

EPDM rubber, GRP fibreglass and felt. Garage roofs, dormers, extensions — guaranteed in writing for up to 25 years.

Lead & flashing

Chimney lead-work, valley and abutment flashings, soakers, code 4–5 dressed in by hand. The work most leaks come back to.

Gutters & fascias

uPVC gutter, fascia and soffit renewal. Cast-iron repairs on period properties. Annual clear-and-check plans available.

Emergency repairs

Storm damage, sudden leaks, missing tiles. On-site same day across Middlesbrough, Stockton, Redcar and the wider Teesside area.

Council & commercial

Schedule-of-rates contracts, school and community-building reroofs, RAMS and full H&S compliance. Three decades of public-sector work.

The work

Roofs you can drive past in twenty years and not flinch.

Most of what we do is the work nobody ever sees: properly dressed lead, breathable membrane, double-batten lap, ventilation that meets BS 5250. The bit on top is the easy part.

Every job gets a written scope before work starts, a daily site clean-down, and a sign-off walk-around when we hand it back. Council clients see the same paperwork as homeowners — because the standard is the same.

  • Schedule-of-rates contracts with local authorities
  • Listed-building lead and slate repairs
  • Insurance-claim storm damage
  • Domestic re-roofs across TS postcodes
