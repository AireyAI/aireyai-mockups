What we do

Four things, done very well.

We're not a salon that tries to do everything. We do nails — and we do them with hospital-grade hygiene, premium products, and the kind of finish that lasts three full weeks.

01

Nail Extensions & Overlays

Acrylic, gel and BIAB — any shape, any length. Built on tip, sculpted, or strengthened over your natural nail. Three-week wear guaranteed.

02

Manicures & Pedicures

Express finishes when you're tight on time. Full spa treatments when you're not. Polish, gel, or natural — your call.

03

Nail Art & Design

Chrome, French, ombre, hand-painted detail, occasion sets. Bring a Pinterest screenshot — we'll match it or improve it.

04

Soak-off & Repair

Properly removed — no filing through the natural nail. Broken nail rescue, lifting fix, infill — book it and we'll sort it.

05

Gift Vouchers

In any amount, in the salon or by bank transfer. The birthday, mum, sister, best-friend present that never disappoints.

06

First time in?

New clients get 10% off their first treatment. Buy one, get the second 20% off when you bring a friend.
