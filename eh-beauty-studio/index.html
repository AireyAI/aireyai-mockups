The studio

Just
you and me.

I work one-on-one out of a small studio in Derby. No double-booking, no in and out — just an unhurried hour or two with someone who actually cares whether you like how it sits when you leave.

Treatments are kept small and considered: builder gel, brow lamination, lash lifts and fine line tattoos. Everything I do, I do often.

Treatments

What I do, slowly.

A short, intentional menu. Each treatment runs to its own time — you’ll never feel rushed out of the chair.

i.

BIAB Manicure

Builder gel overlay on natural nails — strengthens, lengthens, lasts. The studio’s signature treatment, booked weeks ahead.

ii.

Gel Polish Manicure

Classic gel polish, prepped and finished cleanly. Long wear, no chips, choose from a wide colour menu.

iii.

Nail Art

Hand-painted detail, chrome, French and freestyle. Bring a reference or trust me — I’ll show you the sketch first.

iv.

Brow Lamination

Fluffy, brushed-up brows that hold for six weeks. Tint and shape included — mapped to your face, not a template.

v.

LVL Lash Lift

Lifts and tints your own lashes for an open-eye finish that lasts. No extensions, no maintenance, no eye-pressure.

vi.

Fine Line Tattoo

Delicate single-needle work — small, considered pieces. Consultation before the booking, never the same day.
