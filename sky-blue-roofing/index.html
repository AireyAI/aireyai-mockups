What we do

Roofing work, done properly, by hand.

Domestic and light commercial across Coventry, Kenilworth, Solihull and the surrounding villages.

New roofs & re-roofs

Full strip-and-replace in slate, concrete tile, clay tile or modern composite. Breathable membrane, new battens, redressed lead.

  • Free roof survey + drone photos
  • Scaffolding included in price
  • 10-year workmanship guarantee

Flat roofs & GRP

Extensions, garages, dormers — torch-on felt, EPDM rubber, or fibreglass GRP. Up to 25-year manufacturer guarantee on materials.

  • Approved GRP installers
  • Insulated overlay options
  • One-day turnaround on garages

Repairs & gutters
