About KPM

Local scaffolders, properly trained, in & out clean.

KPM Scaffolding Ltd is a family-run firm based in Loughborough, working across the whole of Leicestershire. Kelly and the team have been erecting scaffolds for over 30 years — extensions, new builds, renovations, refurbs, re-roofing, plastering, rendering — domestic or commercial, we put them up the same way: properly.

We're CISRS-trained, CITB certified, SSIP & Safecontractor approved, IPAF operators, and fully covered by Public Liability Insurance. Every job is signed off, every lift handed over with a tag, every site swept on the way out. The phone is answered by someone who's been up the ladder, not a call centre.
