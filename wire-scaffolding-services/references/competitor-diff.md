# Competitor scan — Warrington / Cheshire scaffolding

## Wire Scaffolding (current — wirescaffolding.co.uk)

- Generic one-page layout, dated typography, no visual hierarchy
- SSL expired — instant trust kill in Chrome
- Copy is good (family, safety, zero accidents) but design doesn’t earn it
- Phone buried in header; no strong hero CTA

## Category patterns (regional scaffolders)

1. **Stock photos + blue corporate** — interchangeable, no local identity
2. **Phone-first headers** — works for trades; often ugly
3. **Safety badges** (CISRS, NASC) — rarely designed well; opportunity for designed trust chips
4. **Weak mobile** — thumb-zone CTAs missing on most

## Differentiation for mockups

- Lead with **phone + free quote** above the fold (both mocks)
- Make **zero accident / family-run** visible as designed chips, not paragraph text
- **Warrington + Cheshire** in hero for local SEO feel
- Photography-forward hero (not gradient slop) — real scaffold context
- HTTPS-ready positioning: site should feel *secure* before we fix their cert in outreach narrative
